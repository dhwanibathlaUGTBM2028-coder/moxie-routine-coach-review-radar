# Pitch Summary

## Business Problem

Moxie's monetizable problem is routine mismatch. Customers may choose the wrong product or use the right product incorrectly, which creates avoidable bad reviews, lower repeat purchase, and support load.

For Indian haircare, the issue is especially nuanced because hair type, density, oily or flaky scalp, humidity, wash habits, and styling routines all change the product experience.

## What The Tool Does

Moxie Routine Coach + Review Radar connects pre-purchase and post-purchase intelligence:

- Before purchase, it recommends a better-fit routine and explains usage.
- After purchase, it classifies reviews and comments into actionable patterns.
- Weekly, it converts those patterns into product-page fixes, support replies, education ideas, and content ideas.

## Why Moxie Should Care

Not every negative review means the formula failed. Some reviews signal over-application, wrong product fit, unclear routine order, or mismatched expectations in humid weather.

If those patterns are detected early, Moxie can respond with:

- Better product-page quantity visuals
- Hair-type fit guidance
- FAQ updates
- Post-purchase WhatsApp tips
- Short Reels showing application amount and order
- Support templates that troubleshoot before refund or churn

## Suggested Weekly Workflow

1. Every Monday, upload reviews/comments from Amazon, Nykaa, website, Instagram, and support.
2. Review Radar classifies all incoming feedback.
3. Marketing checks top repeated confusion patterns.
4. Website team updates FAQs/product pages.
5. Content team creates usage education reels.
6. Support team uses suggested reply templates.
7. Product team reviews repeated formula or fit concerns.
8. Growth team tracks whether ratings and objections improve over time.
